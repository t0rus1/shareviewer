Generate selected All-Tables

You may want to generate (or re-generate) All-Tables for selected Shares. For example when wanting to repair lost or damaged All-Tables or even, for speed reasons, to simply work with a subset of shares.

How to

  • Make sure you have already set your trading dates on the calendars of the main page. The usual other conditions for generating All-Tables also apply such as ensuring the Datafiles are listed.
  • Select one or more shares from the Shares list on the left hand side.
  • You may save this selection list as a named ShareList for quick and easy recall later - see the Save and Load option just above the big button at the bottom right.
  • The named Sharelist mentioned above may also be applied on the Overview page to limit the Overview to just those selected shares.
  • Many such named Sharelists can be saved. They will survive the re-generation of new Sharelists over time as well, since the share numbers are not saved, only the names of the shares.
  • Click the 'Generate All-Tables for Selected Shares' button

The new All-Tables will be rebuilt relatively quickly. (the more shares you select, the longer it will take of course). Note that existing calculation parameters get applied.

Lastly, note that it is perfectly safe to do this and has relatively few side effects. Just be careful though to remember that all other existing All-Tables remain as is. If you work this way on an ongoing basis, older, ignored shares' All-Tables will become more and more out-dated. Also, these older All-Tables may be sitting with calculation results from earlier parameters.
